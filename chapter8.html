Information Security and Cyber Law

Information security involves protecting information and information systems from unauthorized access, use, disclosure, disruption, modification, or destruction. Key aspects include:

  • Data Integrity: Ensuring that data is accurate and reliable.
  • Data Privacy: Protecting personal information from unauthorized access.
  • Access Control: Restricting access to information systems to authorized users.
  • Incident Response: Preparing for and responding to security breaches.

Cyber law refers to the legal regulations that govern the use of the internet and digital communications. Understanding these laws is essential for:

  • Ensuring compliance with legal standards.
  • Protecting rights in the digital space.
  • Addressing issues related to cybercrime and online privacy.
